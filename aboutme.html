HOW I WORK

I aim for the stars, seeking the boldest, most ambitious solutions to create products that truly transform and inspire. But I also know that even if we land on the Moon, we’ve achieved something extraordinary, laying the foundation to go further.

01

VISION & IDEATION

I create a bold product vision by leveraging user insights, market research, and collaboration with cross-functional teams. I lead brainstorming sessions to generate innovative ideas, prioritize them for impact and feasibility, and align them with strategic goals.

02

STRATEGIC ROAD MAPPING & VALIDATION

I build strategic roadmaps aligned with business goals, securing stakeholder buy-in and validating concepts through rapid experiments or MVPs. I identify and mitigate risks early, ensuring the roadmap is inspiring, data-driven, and execution-ready

03

EXECUTION & TEAM LEADERSHIP

I lead diverse teams to deliver exceptional products by aligning efforts, resolving blockers, and fostering creative problem-solving. I ensure quality by working closely on UX and QA, driving seamless execution, and focusing on delivering a delightful user experience.

04

LAUNCH & BEYOND

I collaborate with marketing, sales, and support to drive cohesive product launches. Post-launch, I analyze key metrics, iterate based on feedback, and lead continuous improvements. I scale products with impactful updates, ensuring sustained growth and business alignment.
